Chromebook Deployment

SCOPE

The primary objective of this project was to purchase and deliver 200 managed Chromebooks to a newly established middle school. The key goal was to balance quality with a limited budget while ensuring the devices supported seamless student learning. Additionally, the project aimed to complete onsite setup within three days, ensuring proper enrollment and compliance with Board of Education standards.

Deliverables

  • 200 Managed Chromebooks: Acquired and configured to meet the educational needs of the students.
  • Onsite Setup and Enrollment: Completed within three days, including device configuration, enrollment, and compliance checks.
  • Training and Support: Provided to the school's staff for ongoing management and troubleshooting.

Challenges

  • Software Deployment Issues: Although the software was successfully tested prior to the site visit, it failed to deploy onsite, necessitating manual enrollment of several Chromebooks.
  • Time Constraints: The project required completion within a tight three-day window.
  • Budget Limitations: Ensuring high-quality devices and extended warranties while staying within the limited budget.

Results

The project was completed ahead of schedule and within the allocated budget. The successful deployment and setup of the Chromebooks resulted in high satisfaction from the stakeholders. This success led to an additional networking project for the school, highlighting the effectiveness and reliability of the project management approach.

  • Thorough Pre-Deployment Testing: Emphasized the importance of extensive testing in environments similar to the deployment site to avoid unforeseen issues.
  • Flexible Risk Management: Demonstrated the value of having a robust risk management plan, including budget cushions and extended timelines.
  • Team Collaboration: Highlighted the importance of having multiple team members onsite to quickly address challenges.
  • Stakeholder Communication: Reinforced the need for clear and continuous communication with stakeholders to manage expectations and ensure satisfaction.
