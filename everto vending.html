Everto Vending

Scope

Create a sustainable and profitable vending business. Acquire neccessary equipment, secure prime locations, build a client base, and ensure compliance with all relevant regulations. The project will involve scouting for locations, online marketing efforts, acquiring necessary equipment and materials, and applying for grants to support the business financially.

Challenges

  • Managing financial resources effectively to cover all necessary expenses.
  • Navigating the application process and meeting eligibility requirements for grants.
  • Ensuring consistent availability of products to meet customer demand and avoid stockouts.

Project Management Approach: Waterfall Method

    Initiation: Defined project objectives include securing high-traffic locations, implementing an online marketing strategy, acquiring and stocking vending machines, and applying for grants.

    Planning: Developed a comprehensive plan covering location scouting, marketing, equipment procurement, and grant application, along with a risk management strategy.

    Execution: Acquired three prime locations, launched the online marketing strategy, installed and stocked vending machines, and initiated grant applications.

    Monitoring: Continuously tracked progress, managed financials, monitored inventory, and ensured regulatory compliance to keep the project on track.

    Closing: Completed the project by reviewing achievements, documenting lessons learned, and preparing a final report on outcomes and performance.

RESULTS

  • Successfully acquired three prime locations within one week of scouting.
  • Achieved 40% more than the expected monthly income.
  • Completed all deliverables except for obtaining grants.

Deliverables

  • Identify and secure high-traffic locations for vending machines.
  • Develop and implement an online marketing strategy to attract clients and promote the business.
  • Purchase and install vending machines and stock them with products.
  • Research and apply for relevant grants to support business operations and expansion.
  • Establish and maintain a risk management plan for the duration of the project.
